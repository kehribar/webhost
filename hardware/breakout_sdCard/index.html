SD card breakout board

Simple breakout for SD cards. One side of the board has footprint for a full SD card and the other side has a micro SD card footprint. I use Elm-Chan's FatFs or Petit FatFs ,depending on the flash/sram of the uC, library when I develop SD card based applications.
